Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Innovation is the process of creating new ideas, solutions, or technologies to drive progress and improvement.

Sustainability

Sustainability involves making decisions and practices that preserve resources and support long-term ecological balance.

Infrastructure

Infrastructure refers to the fundamental systems and structures necessary for the functioning of society, such as roads, utilities, and buildings.

Empowerment

Empowerment is the process of giving individuals or communities the authority, resources, and confidence to control their own lives.

The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.
